Pool Procedures

Note: The pool controller and its auxiliaries include electronics equipment that could be damaged by voltage surges that may be generated in the event of storm and localised lightning strikes. A surge suppressor has been fitted to protect the controller but in the likely event of thunderstorm it is mandatory to switch off the MCB labeled Piscine at the power distribution board located in the garage. When the storm has passed the power can be restored. Similar precautions are advised for telephony and network router (internet) equipment, that should be disconnected from the mains power source.

1.           To Stop Filtration

1.              Open right hand pool house doors

2.              Remove boxes, etc. to reach the pool controller

3.              Touch the screen once to display the controls

4.              Touch the Filtration button, it will normally be in Auto (green)

5.              Touch the Manual button (colour changes from red to green)

6.              Touch On or Off accordingly, select Off before performing work on the filter (when Off is green, pump is off)

Top--------------------

2.           To Start Filtration

1.              Assumes the filtration is in Manual, either On or Off (green indicates which)

2.              Touch On to run filtration continually

3.              Touch Off to stop filtration

4.              Touch Auto to run filtration according to the pre-programmed times

Top--------------------

3.           To Check/Clear Leaf Filter

1.              Open and remove centre inspection lid at filtration end of pool and put aside for access to filtration compartment

2.              Lift out Leaf Filter by its handle

3.              Hold top and bottom handles and twist both parts anticlockwise, the lower basket will come away from the top

4.              Remove debris content; large objects, leaves, etc.

5.              Reconnect top to lower basket and twist both clockwise until locked together

6.              Replace Leaf Filter back into compartment

7.              Replace inspection lid

Top--------------------

4.           To Add a Chlor Shock

1.              Open right hand pool house doors

2.              Locate Chlorine container, jug, and an old sock

3.              Remove lid from Chlorine container by holding it and rotating lid anticlockwise

4.              Remove reusable tie from plastic bag – do this in open air and avoid breathing the fumes

5.              List out plastic bag and measure 50 g (about half a mug, one tea cup) of Chlorine

6.              Pour  the Chlorine into the old sock and loosely tie the sock

7.              Reseal the plastic bag using the tie, replace in the container and refit the lid

8.              Perform Sequence 1 To Stop Filtration

9.              Open and remove centre inspection lid at filtration end of pool and put aside for access to filtration compartment.

10.          Place sock of Chlorine into the Leaf Filter

11.          Replace inspection lid at filtration end of pool

12.          Wash your hands

13.          Perform Sequence 2 To Start Filtration, see 2.2 to run it continuously for 24 hours

14.          After 24 hours perform Sequence 2 To Start Filtration, see 2.4 to run according to programmed times

Top--------------------

5.           Restart Tripped Filtration Pump

1.              If the pump stops working it may be restarted by switching off/on power supply

2.              Switch off the MCB labelled Piscine at the power distribution board in the garage

3.              Wait for 10 to 15 seconds then switch it back on again

4.              The pool controller should reinitialise and start the pump according to the programmed times

Top--------------------

6.           Perform Suction Cleaning

1.              Perform Sequence 1 To Stop Filtration

2.              Place Suction Cleaner on poolside at shallow end diagonally opposite filtration

3.              Uncoil blue hose and fit to pressure outlet below the waterline at position of first inspection lid

4.              Fit other end to Suction Cleaner

5.              Fit extending pole to Suction Cleaner

6.              Carefully lower Suction Cleaner into shallow end holding both pole upright and the hose until it sits on the bottom of the pool

7.              Perform Sequence 2 to Start Filtration in manual mode

8.              Carefully push and pull Suction Cleaner on bottom of pool using extending pole; ensure Suction Cleaner stays upright and on bottom of pool

9.              When finished, return Suction Cleaner to shallow end and Perform Sequence 1 To Stop Filtration

10.          Lift out onto side reversing step 6

11.          Disconnect hose and extending pole

12.          Remove yellow locking pin to open Suction Cleaner, and clean debris out with hose and clean water

Top--------------------
