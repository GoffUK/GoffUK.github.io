War Diary of Frank George GOFF

F. G. Goff

2 Summerleaze Road

MAIDENHEAD

Sapper F. G. Goff

Royal Engineers

Monday 8 May 1916 - Left Llandudno - Arrived at Avonmouth

Wednesday 10 May 1916 - Went by train to Devonport

Friday 12 May 1916 - Sailed on H.M.T. City of Chester

Tuesday 16 May 1916 - Passed Gibraltar

Wednesday 24 May 1916 - Arrived at Port Said

Thursday 25 May 1916 - Entered Suez Canal

Friday 26 May 1916 - Entered Red Sea
Tuesday 30 May 1916 - Passed Rocks, Twelve Apostles

Wednesday 31 May 1916  - Entered Gulf of Aden

Sunday 4 June 1916 - Passed Muscat (Gulf of Oman), discharges at entrance to harbour
Wednesday 7 June 1916 - Entered mouth of [river] Shatt Al-arab [just north of Kuwait]

Friday 9 June 1916 - Landed at Magil, marched to Makina Camp [near Basra]

Wednesday 14 June 1916 - Visited Ashar

Friday 23 June 1916 - Admitted to 32nd Blg H with Dysentery

Thursday 6 July 1916 - Embarked on H.M.H.S. Varela [hospital ship]
Passenger liner, British India Steam Navigation Co.
Built 1914. 4645 grt.
Hospital ship, World War I and World War II.
Scrapped 1951.

Wednesday 12 July 1916 - Arrived at Bombay
Thursday 13 July 1916 - Admitted to Calaba War Hospital, Bombay

Sunday 20 August 1916 -  Left Calaba proceeding to Alexandra [now Indira] Docks

Monday 21 August 1966 - Left Bombay
Tuesday 22 August 1916 - Arrived at Hislop Auxiliary War Hospital, Trimilgherry, Secunderabad

Sunday 8 October 1916 - Entered British Convalescent Depot


Thursday 2 November 1916 - Shifted to Gough ?? Barracks

Thursday 21 December 1916 - Left Secunderabad

Saturday 23 December 1916 - Arrived at British Infantry Combined Depot, Bangalore


Now there is a big gap of nearly 8 months, until...
Saturday 18 August 1917 - Left Bangalore

Tuesday 21 August 1917 - Arrived at Bombay and sailed on H.M.T. Edevana

Monday 27 August 1917 - Arrived at and quarantined at Ashar Barracks
18 September 1917 -  Turned out to fire at Ashar at 11pm.

Christmas at Ashar Barracks
  18 January 1918 -  Attached to Engineers at Field Park Barracks

2 March 1918 - Pulled Officer out of shaft at El Arab

19 April 1918 - Granted higher rate of pay at No.5 E.F.P.
  9 June 1918 -  Went to British Convalesence Depot, Mohammerah [modern Khorramshahr, an Iranian port adjacent to the Shatt Al Arab waterway]

23 June 1918 - Returned to E.F.P. Basra
  1 September 1918 - Left Ashar.

2 September 1918 - Arrived at Amara.

Left Amara.

3 September 1918 - Passed Al Garhbi.

4 September 1918 - Passed Sheiks Saad.
  5 September 1918 - Arrived at Kut.

7 September 1918 - Left Kut and proceeded to Hanaidi by train.

8 September 1918 - Travelled to Advance Base and on to Baghdad.
4 October 1918 - Visited Table Mountain [Iraq].

13 December 1918 - Moved up to Qizil Rabat [Iraq].

Xmas 1981 - Qizil Rabat E.F.P.

6 April 1919 - Left Qizil Rabat for xxxxx.

7 April 1919 - Arrived Hxxx and Advance Base.

8 April 1919 - Left Advance Base.

9 April 1919 - Arrived at Kut [Iraq].

10 April 1919 - Left Kut.

11 April 1919 - Arrived at Amara and entrained.

12 April 1919 - Arrived at E.F.P. Basra.

15 April 1919 - Shifted to Demob[ilization] Camp at Magil [Iraq].

27 April 1919 - Embarked and sailed on Franz Ferdinand

2 May 1991 - Disembarked and entrained at Bombay

3 May 1919 - Arrived at Gough Barracks, Secunderabad

21 August 1919 - Arrived at Port Said.

22 August 1919 - Left Port Said.

25 August 1919 - Passed through Straits of Messina.

27 August 1919 - Arrived at Marseille.

28 August 1919 - Disembarked at Marseille and spent night at rest camp.

29 August 1919 - Entrained and proceeded via Lyon, Chalons to Boulogne

30 August 1919 - Went into rest camp for the night.
1 September 1919 - Shifted to another rest camp.

3 September 1919 - Left Astrobase Camp and embarked on Channel Steamer Arundel arriving at Dover and entraining for dispersal area



 4 September 1919 - Arrived at Dinton Dispersal Camp 01.30. Passed through and entrained for home same morning 06:00.
