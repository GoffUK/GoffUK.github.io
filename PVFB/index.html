PVFB

What is PVFB?    

PVFB is the Poloidal Field Vertical Field Boost Amplifier. It is the actuator for the JET vertical field circuit for controlling plasma radial position.

It is designed solely to assist the JET plasma start-up phase. To maintain the radial equilibrium of the plasma column during the early fast rise phase, the voltage applied to the vertical field coils needs to be around half the voltage applied to the magnetising coil (P1), typically 10 kV to 20 kV, depending on the premagnetisation current.

The vertical field circuit of JET (P4) is supplied from an amplifier (PVFA 3-4) rated 2.8 kV no-load, 35 kA for 12.7 seconds. With this limited capability the radial position control will saturate for 150 ms to 300 ms causing a radial position error thus restricting the parameter range for breakdown.
